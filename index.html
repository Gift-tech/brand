THE MEDIA COMPANY- WHO WE ARE.

The Media Company, better known as T.M.C was founded in New York in 2017; in 2019 the company relocated its operation to Lagos, Nigeria (Africa’s Entertainment Capital) and has expanded from functioning as a Media Company which also include a Talent Management Agency, Advertising Consulting firm, Media House, Liaison management services, a subsidiary production company (PSA Multimedia). TMC has established itself as a powerhouse in both entertainment and marketing, impacting the industry while keeping its identity and showing the importance of social responsibility. We are an independent company in a third world country that has sold millions of records, built an incredible brand, maintained and built a data base of partners, acquaintances and friends in every and all industries, and inked major endorsement deals (Etisalat, Hennessey, Samsung, Coca Cola, Pepsi, Ciroc, MTN Star to name a few). In addition, TMC has over the years built a portfolio of event management and production with a track record of creating, managing, Liaising, producing and/or partnering in the execution of events and campaigns for reputable brands such as Oando Plc., Samsung West Africa, `DSTV, Coca-Cola and Diamond Bank Plc.
